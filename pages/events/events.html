SHPE Nationals


The annual SHPE National Convention is the largest technical and career conference for Hispanics in STEM the country.

Each year, the SHPE Convention attracts over 6,000 STEM students, professionals and corporate representatives. The convention is an opportunity for leading corporations, government agencies, and other organizations to recruit top STEM talent. The convention also provides educational, technical, competitive and other development opportunities for professionals and students in STEM.
