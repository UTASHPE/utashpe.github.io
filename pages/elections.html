Alexia Acosta

I have had the honor to serve as the community outreach coordinator, Vice President, and President for SHPE UTA. As the community outreach coordinator, I planned at least one community outreach event every month and encouraged members to attend the events. I also began the first collaboration with Trinity SHPE Jr. and Lockheed Martin HOLA and SHPE AERO to plan a community outreach event. By serving as the Lead Coordinator for HEB ISD ACE Science Day, there has been an increase of students and volunteers attending ACE Science Day. In 2017, 65 students and 25 volunteers attended the event, and several professionals from Lockheed Aero believed it had been the smoothest event they have had. In 2018, the event was even more successful than the previous year, and 105 students and 35 volunteers attended. Both events were planned under a rigorous budget of $500.00, but by leading a successful budget management plan, both events were exactly on budget. This collaboration of participating with Trinity SHPE Jr. Chapter and Lockheed Martin and SHPE Aero have resulted in being awarded the Blue Heart Chip Award, which is awarded by SHPE National, in RLDC 2017 and RLDC 2018.

As president, I have focused in rebuilding our relationship with SHPE DFW Chapter which has resulted in meeting professionals and corporate representatives who are interested in investing in SHPE UTA or help SHPE UTA members obtain employment/internships in their companies. I have also attended all of events hosted by Lockheed Martin SHPE Aero, and this relationship could potentially lead to more opportunities to help members get hired or prepared for interviews as well as possibly obtain more funding to help more members attend the National Conference. I have also focused in seeking to work with more engineering and Hispanic organizations that have similar goals as SHPE like I did with the Microsoft Tech Talk with NSBE.
